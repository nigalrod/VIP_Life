Presidential Suites in Boca Chica is a unique and incomparable place where you can enjoy time with your spouse, spend time with friends or live a high end Caribbean experience with your family.

Air-conditioning, coffee maker, cable TV, telephone, Radio-Alarm clock radio, hair dryer iron with board and laundry service($). A mini refrigerator includes water, beer and soft drinks refreshed daily at no extra charge. Balcony or Terrace. Wi-Fi and Safety Deposit Box available($)
